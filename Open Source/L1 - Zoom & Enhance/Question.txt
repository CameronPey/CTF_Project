# 🕵️ OSINT Challenge: Zoom & Enhance

Someone left behind a photo… but it's more than just pixels. 🖼️  
Using only the image provided, you'll need to dig deep and uncover the details:  
**Where** was it taken? **Who** posted it? **When** did it happen?

All the answers are out there — you just have to follow the digital breadcrumbs. 🌐

## 🔍 Questions

1. **What city** was this photo taken in?  
2. **What street** is the restaurant on?  
3. **What is the username** of the Yelp user who posted the photo?  
4. **What year** was the photo posted on Yelp?
